FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know about working with us. Can't find what you're looking for? Drop us a line.

How much does a website cost?

A complete website project costs £1,000, 50% paid upfront, and 50% paid on completion before delivery. This includes custom design, development, SEO setup, and launch. An optional maintenance plan is available for £200 every 4 weeks for ongoing updates, security patches, priority support, tailored direction and branding, page updates, and lots more.

How long does it take to build a website?

For small to medium-sized businesses, websites are typically completed within around 2 weeks, depending on the scope of requirements and how quickly feedback is provided. We'll give you a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with local businesses?

Absolutely. Nuvisco specialises in helping small and local businesses create a more professional online presence. We're based in Cambridge and Cambridgeshire and work with businesses across the UK.

What industries do you work with?

We work with a wide range of industries, particularly: local service businesses, tradespeople, automotive and detailing companies, professional services, hospitality, beauty and lifestyle brands. Each project is assessed individually to ensure we're the right fit.

Will my website rank on Google?

No company can guarantee Google rankings. However, Nuvisco builds websites with SEO best practices baked in — strong semantic structure, performance optimisation, Core Web Vitals, and content organisation — all designed to improve your search engine visibility.

Do I need a new website or a redesign?

Nuvisco focuses on creating custom websites from the ground up. This gives us full control over design, performance, functionality, and user experience. If you have existing branding, we can incorporate it into the new build.

What makes a website convert visitors into customers?

High-converting websites typically include: clear messaging, strong calls-to-action, mobile optimisation, fast loading speeds, trust signals (reviews, testimonials), and simple navigation. We design every site with these principles in mind.

Can you redesign my website while keeping my branding?

Yes. Existing branding elements such as logos, colours, fonts, images, and business identity can be incorporated into a new website. We'll work with what you have to create something fresh and effective.

Do I need a website if I already get customers from referrals?

Even if you're busy through referrals, a professional website helps you establish credibility, attract new customers, and scale beyond word-of-mouth. It's your digital storefront — open 24/7.

What platforms do you build websites with?

Nuvisco creates custom websites from scratch using vanilla JavaScript, Three.js, GSAP, and modern CSS. This gives us — and you — full control over design, performance, and functionality without the limitations of templates.

How do I know if my website is outdated?

Signs your website may need updating: it looks outdated compared with competitors, is difficult to navigate, doesn't work well on mobile, isn't generating enquiries, or lacks modern design standards. If any of these sound familiar, we can help.
